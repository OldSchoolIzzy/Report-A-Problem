Aurora University Report A Problem

Welcome to the Campus Problem Reporting Application! Here, students and faculty can easily report any issues or concerns they have on campus, whether it be related to Wi-Fi issues or maintenance. With this application, you can quickly and easily notify the appropriate authorities of any problems you encounter, ensuring that they are addressed in a timely manner. Whether you see a broken light, a leaking faucet, or any other issue, please feel free to use this application to report it and help us keep our campus safe and well-maintained. Thank you for using the Campus Problem Reporting Application!

   1. Click on the REPORT tab located on the top of the page

   2. From the report page, select the category that best describes your issue

   3. Selecting the category will bring you to a new page, in this page select the building, room, proximity to the room and sub issue from the drop down menus

   4. This step is optional, to add an image, click on add image button and select the photo you will like to attach. This application only allows jpeg format photos

   5. Click Submit to submit the report!

   6. To view previosuly created tickets you, click on the TICKET tab on the top of the page

   7. To locate the correct building name refer the campus map found here
